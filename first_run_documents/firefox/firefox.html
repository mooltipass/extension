Thanks for downloading the Mooltipass Extension

It is strongly recommended to disable all other password management applications to prevent potential incompatibilities with the Mooltipass ecosystem.

Part 1: Disabling 3rd Party Password Managers

Step 1

Click the menu icon (☰) on the top right corner of your browser. In the drop down menu that appears, choose "Add-ons". In the new tab that appears, select "Extensions" in the left side menu. You should see all of the Firefox add-ons installed.


Step 2

Find other password managing applications, remove them by clicking the dedicated button:

Be sure to repeat these steps for all other password extensions.

Part 2: Disabling Firefox's Password Manager

You might have noticed that Firefox asks you if you would like to store credentials, even if you have not installed any password management extensions.

Step 1:

Click the menu icon (☰) on the top right corner of the browser. In the drop down menu that appears, choose "Options".


Step 2

In the page that appears, click the "Privacy and Security" item in the left side menu. Scroll down until you find the "Logins & Passwords" section and uncheck "Ask to save logins and passwords for websites":

Congratulations, you have correctly setup the Mooltipass extension!
